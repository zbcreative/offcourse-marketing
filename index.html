What's inside

The complete golf
improvement ecosystem.

Most golf apps stop at the scorecard. Offcourse is where you track and schedule practice: range sessions, workouts, drills, mental exercises and scoring data so you can see real progress and keep improving when you're off the course.

Practice logging

Log range and short game in seconds: balls hit, clubs worked, time on task, and how the session felt. Your volume and mix stay visible week over week.

Workouts & drills

60+ coach-style drills and 30+ golf fitness workouts with setup, cues, and progressions. Log a session and earn XP when you finish.

Mental training

20 mental exercises across visualization, breathing, and pressure. Mental sessions earn XP and count toward streaks like everything else.

Round tracking

Record score, fairways, GIR, putts, and up & downs for every round. Personal bests update automatically and feed your insights.

Practice & round calendar

Schedule practice and rounds ahead of time so training is a plan, not a scramble. Build a routine that fits real life.

Stats & insights

Monthly activity by session type, practice by club, and round averages. See exactly how your practice off the course is impacting your performance on the course.

How it works

Simple to log.
Hard to stop.

1
Set your handicap goal
Tell Offcourse where you are and where you want to be. Every feature is built around closing that gap.
2
Log every session
Track range sessions, short game, workouts, mental exercises, and rounds in a few taps. Use the training calendar to schedule practice in advance so improvement is a plan, not a last-minute guess. When you log consistently, you see what is working.
3
Earn XP and climb tiers
Every logged session earns XP. Watch your tier rise from Weekend Warrior toward Big Cat, one threshold at a time.
4
Watch your handicap drop
Consistent practice, tracked over time, with the right drills and workouts. That is how handicaps fall.
