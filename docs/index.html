How it works

Simple to log.
Hard to stop.

1
Set your handicap goal
Tell Offcourse where you are and where you want to be. Every feature is built around closing that gap.
2
Log every session
Track range sessions, short game, workouts, and drills in a few taps. When you log practice consistently, you see what is working.
3
Earn XP and climb tiers
Every logged session earns XP. Watch your tier rise from Weekend Warrior toward Big Cat, one threshold at a time.
4
Watch your handicap drop
Consistent practice, tracked over time, with the right drills and workouts. That is how handicaps fall.
