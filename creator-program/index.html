Creator program

We built Offcourse for golfers who care about the game on and off the course. The creator program is for anyone who makes golf content, at any audience size, on any platform. YouTube long form, Instagram Reels, TikTok, Shorts, podcasts, newsletters, or a mix. If golf is what you talk about, you belong here.

Who it is for

You create videos or media about golf. That might be course vlogs, swing tips, gear reviews, comedy, travel, instruction, practice logs, or something else entirely. We are not looking only for “practice only” creators. Small account or large, we care that you show up authentically and are willing to give straight feedback while we ship.

What you get

  • Early access to builds and features before public release
  • Talking points, screenshots, and product context you can use in your own voice
  • A direct line for bugs, ideas, and “this is confusing on camera” moments
  • Recognition as we grow (we keep track of creators who showed up early)
  • Optional collabs when we run campaigns or want real stories from people who play and post

What we ask

  • Use the product the way you would naturally cover it, not a one-off install
  • Tell us what breaks or what viewers would not understand
  • Label sponsored or gifted access clearly when you post
  • Keep confidential builds private until we say otherwise

Apply

Tell us who you are and how you create. Your email app will open with everything filled in. You need a configured mail client on this device.

We read every application. We add creators in waves as we can support them well.
